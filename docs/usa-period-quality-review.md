# USA Period Quality Review

## Purpose

Execute `usa-task-15` directly: a period-by-period quality judgment over the current USA dataset.

This is a higher-reasoning step because it is deciding where the dataset is already strong enough, where it remains only provisionally strong, and where later effort would genuinely improve the product.

## Review standard

Each period is judged on:
- event grounding
- pressure grounding
- echo usefulness
- snapshot readability
- lived-voice accessibility
- overall exhibit legibility

The goal is not to make every period equally dense.
The goal is to make every period usable, intelligible, and worth exploring.

## Review outcomes

### `us-p01` Revolution and Constitutional Founding

Judgment: `usable, slightly lean`

Why:
- the two anchor events are strong and canonical
- the voice layer works well
- the echoes are useful and conceptually central

Remaining weakness:
- it still feels lighter than later periods on visible event density

Action:
- no immediate change
- keep on watchlist only

### `us-p02` Republic-Building, Expansion, and Sectional Strain

Judgment: `usable, structurally improved`

Why:
- the addition of `us-e19` fixed a real weakness
- the period now reads clearly as expansion plus coercion, not just expansion
- the lived voice is accessible and strong

Remaining weakness:
- echo depth is still weaker than in the best-connected periods

Action:
- no immediate new event
- later watchpoint for echo confidence, not raw quantity

### `us-p05` Depression, New Deal, and World War

### `us-p06` Postwar Order and Civil Rights Transformation

Judgment: `very strong`

Judgment: `usable, interpretively dependent`

Why:

Remaining weakness:
- it still depends more on interpretation than on a naturally self-evident event stack
- compared with `us-p05`, `us-p06`, and `us-p08`, it remains less immediately iconic

Action:
- keep on watchlist for later validation
- no immediate expansion

### `us-p08` Security State, Platform Era, and Polarization

Judgment: `very strong`

Why:
- excellent public legibility
- strong event grounding

Remaining weakness:

Action:
- no content change
- be aware of balance when comparing period salience across the field
