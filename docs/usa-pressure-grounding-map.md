# USA Pressure Grounding Map

## Purpose

Record how the first USA seed layer grounds the shared pressure model in concrete anchor events.

## Highest-priority grounded pressures

### Institutional legitimacy

Grounded by:
- Constitution and Bill of Rights
- 14th Amendment
- Civil Rights Act
- Voting Rights Act
- Watergate scandal
- United States presidential election of 2016

### Inequality

Grounded by:
- Mexican-American War and Treaty of Guadalupe Hidalgo
- Emancipation Proclamation
- 14th Amendment
- Sherman Antitrust Act
- Plessy v. Ferguson
- Civil Rights Act
- Financial crisis of 2007-08

### Information acceleration

Grounded by:
- Declaration-era public print politics
- United States presidential election of 1968
- Watergate scandal
- United States presidential election of 2016

### Militarization

Grounded by:
- Declaration of Independence
- Mexican-American War
- Emancipation Proclamation / Civil War context
- Day of Infamy and entry into World War II
- September 11 attacks
